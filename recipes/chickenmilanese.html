Chicken Milanese

Description

I made this chicken Milanese after I couldn't find a recipe that would work with pasta. It's crispy, quick to make, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup italian crumbs
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 lemon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200
  2. Beat eggs
  3. Press chicken gently
  4. fry chicken in vegegable oil
  5. Serve with lemon wedges
