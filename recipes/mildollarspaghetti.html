Million Dollar Spaghetti

Description

It's a million dollar spaghetti, who wouldn't want one?!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup italian crumbs
  • spaghetti stuff
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 lemon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 200
  2. Beat eggs
  3. Cook spaghetti
  4. fry spaghetti in vegegable oil
  5. Serve with lemon wedges
